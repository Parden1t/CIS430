Welcome aboard everyone. We are happy to be here and assist you in your chocolate needs. We are a family business with a track record of over ten years in the chocolate industry. We are now pleased to introduce you with our most delicious selection of gourmet chocolates. Each batch is prepared with the highest quality ingredients by our best chefs. We provide small orders and special orders for our friends in the service industry.

We are pleased to announce that the cocoa we use is harvested and shipped from Ecuador. Only the highest standards are accepted at chocolatina. The milk that is used for the chocolate is from Alpine cows and provides a smooth silky texture. This picture was taken at one of our own plantation sites in Ecuador.

We have a variety of creme filled chocolates popular for any occasion. We also have our new gourmet section specially tailored with current popular flavors. We have raspberry delight, chocolate bomb, and salted almonds. We are here to serve you.
